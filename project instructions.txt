
Project Name: Car and home scenary


Project Description:

There are two cars and some homes and trees in the scenario. 
A Sun on blue sky and a moon on black sky (night mode).
Cars are driven on the road and the clouds on the sky will be floating 
when the left mouse button is clicked and also when 'n' is pressed 
the night mode is on and the moon will be shown on the night sky.
But the runniing of two cars and the floating of clouds will be unchanged.
The right mouse button will stop the dynamic scenario.

Instructions:

'n' : night mode is on.
'd' : day mode is on.
'left mouse button' : scenario will be dynamic.
'right mouse button': stops the dynamic scenario.
